    <h3>Variable Name Rules</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Must begin with letter</li>
        <li>Only letters, numbers, and underscores</li>
        <li>Descriptive names</li>
        <li>snake_case</li>
        <li>Reserve Traditional Names</li>
        <li>Avoid Keywords</li>
        <li>Longer > Shorter</li>
